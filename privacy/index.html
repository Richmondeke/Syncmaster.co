Privacy Policy for SyncMaster


Effective Date: July 20, 2025

At SyncMaster, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website, use our platform, or interact with our services.


1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

a. Personal Information

  • Name, email address

  • Artist or producer name

  • Payment details (handled via secure third-party processors)

  • Music catalog metadata (e.g., track titles, ownership splits)

b. Usage Data

  • Pages visited, time spent, device information, browser type

  • IP address and location (approximate)

  • Submission history and platform interactions

c. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience, analyze traffic, and offer personalized content. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings.


2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our services

  • Facilitate music submissions and sync placements

  • Communicate with you about your account, opportunities, and updates

  • Process payments and manage financial transactions

  • Comply with legal obligations and prevent fraud


3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal data.

We may share information with:

  • Sync project clients (e.g., music supervisors) when you submit music

  • Payment processors (e.g., Stripe, PayPal) for transactions

  • Service providers who help us maintain the platform

  • Legal or regulatory authorities, if required by law


4. Data Storage and Security

We use secure, industry-standard practices to protect your data. All data is stored using encrypted connections, and access is limited to authorized personnel only.



5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access or update your personal information

  • Request deletion of your account and data

  • Object to certain types of processing

  • Withdraw consent at any time

You can exercise these rights by contacting us at: hello@syncmaster.live


6. Children’s Privacy

SyncMaster is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13 (or the equivalent age of digital consent in your region). We do not knowingly collect data from children.



7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted here, and we encourage you to review it periodically.


8. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this policy, reach out to:

SyncMaster Support Team
Email: hello@syncmaster.live
